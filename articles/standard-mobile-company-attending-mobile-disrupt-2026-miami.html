If you’re an ITAD company, recommerce operator, or enterprise asset manager attending the show, we want to meet you.

Why We’re Going

Mobile Disrupt brings together the biggest names across the mobile device lifecycle—carriers, ITAD firms, recommerce platforms, and reverse logistics operators—all under one roof for two days of keynotes and, more importantly, real conversations. For a company built around one promise—professional refurbishing at the lowest cost on the planet—this is exactly the room we want to be in.

We’re not coming to Miami to pitch from a stage. We’re coming to sit down with operators who have inventory they can’t profitably process, backlogs they can’t clear, or grade B and C lots that keep bleeding value into liquidation. If that’s you, a fifteen-minute conversation at the show could change your unit economics.

What We Want to Talk About

Since our merger with Vast Repair, our facility in Beihai, China gives us the capacity and cost structure to take on the hard stuff other refurbishers turn away. At Mobile Disrupt, we’d love to walk you through:

  • Hard-to-repair and mixed-condition lots — the units that don’t pencil out for domestic refurbishment but still carry real recoverable value
  • High-volume throughput — 50,000+ units per month of combined processing capacity across mobile, tablet, and computing categories
  • Transparent, per-unit pricing — send us your list and get a full quote in 72 hours, including logistics, parts, repair, and projected yield
  • Compliance you can stand behind — R2 compliant processes, certified data destruction, and warranty-backed output
Bring your toughest lot to the conversation. The units everyone else says no to are exactly the ones we built this company to handle.

Let’s Set a Time

Calendars fill up fast at a show like this, so if you’d like to connect in Miami, reach out before the doors open. Send us your product list ahead of time and we can come to the conversation with real numbers already in hand—no guesswork, no runaround.

Whether you’re rethinking your refurbishment strategy, evaluating an offshore processing partner, or just want to understand how the Standard Mobile–Vast Repair pipeline works, we’d be glad to talk. See you at the Fontainebleau.
